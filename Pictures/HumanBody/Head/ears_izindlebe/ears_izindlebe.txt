Ears : Izindlebe
1.Walls have ears :izindonga zinezindlebe
2.He has big ears :Unezindlebe ezinkulu
3.my ear is itchy :Indlebe yami iyaluma
4.Use your ears :sebenzisa izindlebe zakho
5.You have sharp ears : Unezindlebe ezibukhali
6.keep it to your ear :Yigcine endlebeni yakho
7.My ear hurts:Izindlebe zami zibuhlungu
8.she whispered into my ear : Uhlebele endlebeni yami
9.She pierced her ears :Ubhoboze izindlebe zakhe
10.We hear with our ears : Sizwa ngezindlebe zethu

Deaf: ukungezwa 
1.He / she is deaf : akezwa
2. Are you deaf?:Awuzwa yini?
3.I am not deaf : Ngiyezwa
4.He pretends to be deaf:Wenza ngathi akezwa
5. They are all deaf : Bonke abezwa
